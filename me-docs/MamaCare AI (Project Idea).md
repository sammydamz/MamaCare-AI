MamaCare AI (Project Idea)

MamaCare AI is a voice-based platform that delivers continuous maternal care through simple phone calls, while equipping healthcare workers with real-time dashboards and AI-driven tools to monitor, detect risks, and manage both pregnancy and post-pregnancy loss cases efficiently.

 

Key Features

**1\. Features for Pregnant Women**

**🩺 Health Monitoring**

* Regular **automated voice calls** \*  
* Symptom tracking (bleeding, pain, fever, etc.)  
* Pregnancy stage tracking  
   

**⚠️ Early Risk Detection**

* AI identifies danger signs early  
* Predicts complications (e.g., preeclampsia, miscarriage risk) \*Risk First \*\*Complication Prediction need further

**📚 Health Guidance**

* Personalized (Nutrition included) health advice  
* Medication reminders  
* Hospital visit reminders

**🚑 Smart Referrals**

* Alerts sent to healthcare providers  
* Directions to nearby clinics  
* Emergency escalation

 

 

**2\. Features for Post-Pregnancy Loss Care**

**💬 Emotional Support**

* Compassionate voice interactions  
* Regular check-ins to reduce isolation  
* Mental health support prompts

**🩺 Physical Recovery Monitoring**

* Tracks bleeding and recovery progress  
* Screens for infection or complications

**⚠️ Risk Detection After Loss**

* Identifies warning signs (e.g., sepsis, heavy bleeding)  
* Triggers urgent care referrals

**🌱 Future Care Support**

* Family planning guidance  
* Preparation for next pregnancy  
* Fertility awareness

**📊 Dashboard & Monitoring**

* View all patients and risk levels  
* Track symptoms and engagement

**🚨 Real-Time Alerts**

* Notifications for high-risk cases  
* Emergency escalation alerts

 

 

**📋 Case Management**

* Analytics on patient trends  
* High-risk case tracking

**5\. Accessibility & System Features**

* 📞 Works on **basic phones (no smartphone needed)**  
* 🌐 Supports **local languages**  
* 🔔 Automated call scheduling  
* 🔐 Secure data storage & privacy  
* 📡 Low-bandwidth operation

 

**Smart AI Features (Core Engine)**

* 🧠 **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** for voice interaction  
* 📊 **Risk prediction models**  
* 🔁 **Adaptive care pathways** (pregnancy → post-loss mode)

* 📈 **Continuous learning & personalization**
