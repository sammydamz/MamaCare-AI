Features for MamaCare   AI

Core Flow
	1.	Women are registered at a clinic or by a community health worker.
	2.	calls the woman regularly in her preferred local language.
	3.	The AI asks simple questions about symptoms, feelings, and pregnancy progress.
	4.	The AI analyzes responses and predicts the woman’s risk level (low, medium, or high).
	5.	The AI provides personalized advice, emotional support, or alerts providers based on risk.
	6.	High-risk cases are escalated to health providers and followed up until resolved.
AI Engine
7. A speech-to-text NLP module converts voice responses and understands local languages.
8. A risk prediction ML model analyzes symptoms, behavior, and history to predict risk.
9. A clinical decision support engine categorizes risk and recommends actions.
10. A learning and improvement module updates the model accuracy from outcomes over time.
CHW Dashboard
11. A prioritized patient queue shows all women sorted by risk level with one-line reason summaries.
12. Real-time risk alerts notify the CHW when a woman’s risk tier is upgraded after a call.
13. Individual patient profiles show call history, reported symptoms, risk trends, and referral records.
14. An action log lets CHWs record visits, calls, referrals, and outcomes to close the care loop.
15. A referral tracker monitors whether referred women attended their facility appointment.
16. A facility directory lists nearby clinics with distance, hours, services, and a click-to-call button.
17. A zone summary shows the CHW’s total caseload, pending visits, and unresolved high-risk cases.
18. A supervisor view lets managers see all CHWs in their area and monitor follow-up rates.
19. A USSD offline mode keeps the dashboard functional without internet connectivity.

For pregnancy loss
20. A structured grief and bereavement module delivers time-phased emotional support calls after pregnancy loss.
21. Peer survivor audio testimonials in local languages are played during grief support calls.
22. A partner and family engagement feature sends optional guidance calls or messages to the woman’s support network.
23.A group chat of peer survivors to help each other cope by interacting.
24.An AI counselor and a link to an actual counselor if the need arises Enhancements Proposed
25. An offline-first data caching system runs a simplified IVR screening when the cloud AI is unreachable.
26. A federated privacy-preserving learning architecture keeps patient data within the country.
27. A local-language speech recognition fine-tuning plan builds a maternal health corpus for model improvement.
28. A facility-level analytics dashboard gives health centers aggregate data on referrals, follow-ups, and symptoms.
Key Principles
29. The platform works on any basic phone using only voice calls — no smartphone or internet required.
30. All data is protected with end-to-end encryption and secure handling protocols.
31. The system supports multiple local African languages for inclusive access.
32. The platform integrates CHWs, healthcare providers, and health facilities into one closed-loop ecosystem.
