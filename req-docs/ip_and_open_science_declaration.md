# **INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND OPEN SCIENCE DECLARATION**

## 1. Ownership of Submitted Work
The MamaCare AI platform, including the source code, training configurations, and localized triage workflows, is the original work of the MamaCare AI development team. The team retains proprietary ownership of the custom dashboard layout and GDF-FT implementation while licensing the system components in accordance with open-science principles.

---

## 2. Open-Source Component Declaration
MamaCare AI is built entirely upon established open-source technologies to ensure transparency, reproducibility, and local hosting capability:

*   **Machine Learning Architecture**: Custom weights are fine-tuned from the open-source `distilbert-base-multilingual-cased` model checkpoint under the Apache 2.0 License.
*   **Libraries and Frameworks**: Powered by open-source modules, including:
    *   Hugging Face `transformers`, `peft`, `setfit`, and `datasets` (Apache 2.0).
    *   React, Vite, Express.js (MIT License).
    *   PostgreSQL Database Adapter (PostgreSQL License).

---

## 3. Dataset License Compliance
The training pipeline ingests open-science datasets (such as the IDinsight Urgency Detection dataset). The team complies with all license terms, ensuring that no proprietary or closed data pools are used without proper authorization, and that the resulting trained weights remain accessible under open repositories.
